A brief history

In June 2015 we began as a hobbyist group in New Delhi. We started with a very few services like Web design and Development, Graphic Designing etc. Over a couple of years, we scaled our services and we moved into Mobile Application development. After serving for some time we decided to provide digital infrastructure for the Start-ups. Currently, we are testing prototypes for IOT, Robotics and Home Automation services. We will start offering them by Mid of 2019.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide Supreme quality Tech Infrastructure for the Businesses/Start-ups and Automate their tasks with the use of IOT, Robotics and Artificial Intelligence. To achieve this we are building a smart AI system that will depict which part of business needs to be Automated in order to increase the productivity.

Unlike others, We have built a large set of Intelligent bots/robots that Monitors and send Notifications about health and functionality of the system.
